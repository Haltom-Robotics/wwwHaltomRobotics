Mission Statement

We at Haltom Robotics strive to instill a sense of creativity and inspire our youth. We will solve problems through the use of the engineering process and gain experience in designing, planning, and programming in a virtual environment. We learn how to apply S.T.E.M. skills into our daily lives and use them in innovative ways to implement new thoughts and ideas. Through sheer determination and perseverance, we help students thrive in an engineering environment and prepare them for the world ahead.
